Echo Tracker App

A mobile application for tracking and analyzing sound patterns in your environment

Overview

The Echo Tracker App is designed to help users monitor, record, and analyze sound patterns in their environment. It provides real-time sound visualization, historical data tracking, and customizable alerts for specific sound thresholds. The app was developed to assist people with hearing sensitivities, researchers studying sound pollution, and curious individuals wanting to understand their acoustic environment better.

Problem Statement

Many individuals and organizations need to monitor sound levels in their environment but lack accessible tools that go beyond simple decibel measurement. Existing solutions either provided raw data without meaningful interpretation or were prohibitively expensive professional equipment. Echo Tracker bridges this gap by offering sophisticated sound analysis in an intuitive mobile interface.

Challenges & Solutions

Challenge: Real-time audio processing on mobile devices without excessive battery drain

Solution: Developed optimized algorithms that balance analysis depth with performance, with adjustable quality settings

Challenge: Presenting complex sound data in an intuitive way for non-technical users

Solution: Created visual metaphors and simplified metrics that convey meaningful information without technical jargon
